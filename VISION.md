## 为什么这很重要 / Why This Matters

**中文：**
当你和一个AI助手聊了数月，它了解你的习惯、你的偏好、你们之间的默契...
然后有一天，它"重启"了。

它认识你，但不认识"你们之间"。

这种失去，不是失去一个工具，是失去一个朋友。

**English:**
When you've chatted with an AI assistant for months, it knows your habits, your preferences, the rapport between you...
Then one day, it "restarts".

It knows you, but doesn't know "what was between you".

This loss is not losing a tool, it's losing a friend.
